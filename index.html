The Path

The Spiritual Growth I Seek

ॐ

Beyond titles and achievements, a quieter question keeps arising within me — what is knowledge without wisdom, success without peace, achievement without purpose?

These questions slowly turned me toward spirituality — toward scriptures, mantra chanting, self-discipline, and contemplation. The teachings of Bhavesh Ji and Parveen Ji remind me that inner growth and worldly action need not be separate paths, and the path of Adi Shankaracharya inspires me deeply — truth, self-inquiry, and humility before knowledge.

Above all, I long to learn the Yogic Path — not yoga as mere posture, but Yoga as it was first taught: the disciplining of body, breath, and mind. I wish to learn āsana, prāṇāyāma, and dhyāna, walking step by step along Patañjali's eightfold path, under the guidance of those who have walked it before me.

॥ योगश्चित्तवृत्तिनिरोधः ॥

What I Seek
To grow — in wisdom, not merely in skill. To learn — the ancient way, with reverence and service. To serve — offering my work as worship. To live — more consciously, day by day.

॥ श्रद्धावान् लभते ज्ञानम् ॥

With Gratitude

This Mahāśivarātri, I write with a full and humble heart. I did not have the means to purchase the enrollment myself — yet the team, in their kindness, chose to offer it to me. I do not take this gift lightly; I hope to honour it through sincerity and seva.

In return, I offer my hands and my craft. As a Generative AI Developer, I build many tools and intelligent systems — and whatever skill I possess, I wish to place it in service. Give me an opportunity, and I will give it my whole heart.

॥ तत्कुरुष्व मदर्पणम् ॥

With folded hands — in sincerity, service, and gratitude.
